## The first light

There is a small corner of the kitchen where the morning sun rests for ten minutes. In that light, ideas arrive softly and without pressure.

## A cup and a page

The practice is simple: boil water, pour tea, write three lines. Those lines have turned into chapters, collaborations, and new friendships.

## Carrying it forward

The ritual is shared now, passed from one storyteller to the next.
