## The circle

A group of friends met once a month for six years. The only rule was to show up with a story, even if the story felt unfinished.

## The vow

By the fourth year, their meetings became a lifeline. They learned that keeping promises to each other was also keeping promises to themselves.

## The invitation

Join the circle, even if the story is unfinished.
